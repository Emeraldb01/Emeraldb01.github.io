Speech Emotion Recognition

Classify speech into different emotions, using an ensemble CNN-LSTM model


The goal of this project is to classify voice audio into "calm", "happy", "sad", "angry", "fearful", "disgusted", "surprised".

We employed the model proposed in 'An ensemble 1D-CNN-LSTM-GRU model with data augmentation for speech emotion recognition' after researched.
Three different models- LSTM, CNN+LSTM, CLSTM are tested.
The LSTM model purly consists of 7 LSTM layers with dropout layers, CNN+LSTM model mix convolution layers with one LSTM layer, and CLSTM includes multiple CNN layers and LSTM layers. In CLSTM, there are three convolutional blocks followed by some LSTM layers.
