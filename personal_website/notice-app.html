NOTICE!

A Web-Based AI-Powered Note-Taking Application

Team Project
Year: 2024 | Role: Full-Stack Developer, AI Integrator

Demo Video

Key Features:
- Real-time course transcription via API for accurate note-taking.
- Automated bullet-point summaries by comparing transcripts with existing notes.

Results

NOTICE addresses a common challenge for university students: missing important points during class. Through real-time transcription and automated summary generation, it ensures well-structured and comprehensive notes, even when users momentarily lose focus.

To design an effective solution, we conducted six two-hour user interviews to evaluate the strengths and weaknesses of existing note-taking applications. Feedback from these sessions guided the development of features that meet students' needs.

Future plans include matching audio recordings to notes, enabling users to revisit specific sections of a lecture seamlessly for better study and review. Additionally, we aim to enhance transcription accuracy and streamline the user experience further.
